Iced Coffee

Chill Zone

For those days when you need a little boost!

This recipe can be caterated to any taste... AS LONG AS YOU LOVE COFFEE!!

Ingredients

  • Cold Brew ( I perfer extra strong)
  • Heavy Cream
  • Half and Half
  • Carmel Drizzle
  • Carmel syrup
  • Crushed Ice
  • Drinking glass

Steps

  1. Coat inside of Glass with carmel drizzle
  2. Fill cup with ice 2/3 to top
  3. Fill cup 1/4 with half and half
  4. Fill cup 2/4 with cold brew
  5. 5 pumps of carmel syrup
  6. Froth heavy cream and pour in top
  7. 5 pumps of carmel syrup
  8. Drizzle carmel ontop of cream
  9. When you're ready to enjoy stir and DRINK!!!
